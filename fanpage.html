Ansel Adams

Bio

Ansel Adams was born on February 20, 1902 to April 22, 1984. He was a landscape photographer and was famous for his black and white photos. At the age of 12 he was given his first camera during his visit to Yosemite National Park

Adams was a life long advocate for environmental conservation, and the photo's that he took brought out this advocacy.

Reasons I like Ansel Adams

  • He is famous for his black and white photos
  • He really captures the beauty of nature
  • He understands how to manipulate a camera
